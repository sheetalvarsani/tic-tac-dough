Tic-Tac-Dough

Welcome to Tic-Tac-Dough!

Welcome to a delicious twist on the classic Tic-Tac-Toe game!

In this carb-themed version, you'll face off against the computer in a battle of baguettes versus bagels.

  • HOW TO PLAY:
  • You start the game with the baguette token, while the computer plays as the bagel.
  • Take turns placing your tokens on the 3x3 grid.
  • The first player to align three tokens in a row, column, or diagonal wins.
  • If the grid is filled without a winner, the game ends in a draw.
